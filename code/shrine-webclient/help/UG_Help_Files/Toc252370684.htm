1.1.2 Data Steward

The Data Steward administrator must review and approve your Query Topic requests before you access SHRINE data. In addition, the Data Steward administrator also regularly reviews your use of the Query Tool to ensure that no unapproved examination of data is performed. You submit a request through the Data Steward application that describes your intended use of the system. Log back in to the application to find out when your request is approved.

You can access the Data Steward application from within the SHRINE Query Tool. Click Request New Topic in the tool view to open the application in a new window, and log in by using your eCommons account credentials.

Or, access the application directly through this link.

Individual queries are subject to review by the Data Steward administrator for compliance with approved topics. Query histories are logged and audited on a regular basis to ensure that there are no violations of the Terms of Access Agreement that you must accept when you log in to SHRINE. Any inappropriate use of the Query Tool to investigate patient data will result in immediate suspension of your account, and notification will be sent to your institution regarding possible misconduct.
